About Us

A second generation shoemakers from Chennai registered with the Film Employees Federation of South India.

Our commitment and expert contributions to the costume designing field dates back to the 1950’s especially in the renowned 1964 historical drama, Karnan starring Nadigar Thilagam Shivaji Ganesan.

We specialize in crafting historical costumes like body armor, arm guards, neck scarf, greaves to protect legs, military shoes, leather shoes and boots. Worked on numerous projects for MGR, NTR, Raj Kumar, ANR, Rajini Kanth, Kamal Hassan

Work was recognized and displayed besides some of the greatest Indian costume designers like Bhanu Athaiya and Neeta lulla, in Hawaii University’s (US) exhibition that showcases 100 years of Indian cinema costumes. Displayed in memorials of Dr. MGR and Shivaji Ganesan
